Check in for your Jobcentre Plus appointment v0.1

A prototype to help the Digital Jobcentres team research people's experiences in checking in for scheduled appointments.

Version 0.1 - Sprint 6

First prototype. Reflects a basic check-in interaction worked out with user researchers and business analysts. Includes scenarios for:

  • has an appointment, is on time (for demo, use birth month from January to April)
  • has an appointment, is late (for demo, use birth month from May to August)
  • details do not match an appointment record - check answers (for demo, use birth month from September to December)
  • cannot find appointment
  • does not have an appointment

Focused prototype on telling users where they stand, including where to wait and showing that staff would be notified that they're there.

Used established patterns from the government Design System as a starting point. These are tried and tested and shown to be accessible and usable across device types. We can adapt if first-hand evidence from user research shows we need to take a different approach for our context.
